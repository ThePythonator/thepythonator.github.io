Information

The Thargoids have returned...
Defend the planet!

Controls

W / UP: Forwards thrust
A / LEFT: Turn left
D / RIGHT: Turn right
SHIFT / B: Upwards thrust
SPACE: Fire weapon

Note: If using SHIFT instead of B, you may need to disable sticky keys!

HUD

At the bottom, three bars show the fuel remaining, altitude, and ammo remaining.
In the top left there is a top-down view of the game map, with a white triangle marking your location, and a red triangle for each enemy within range.
In the top right the number of Thargoids remaining is displayed.

Highscores

Scoring

You are awarded 50 points per Thargoid shot down, but lose 1 point per shot fired. At the end of each successful round, you gain up to 100 bonus points, depending on how much fuel you have remaining.

Environment

Each level will alternate between Earth and the Thargoid's home planet (which looks remarkably similar to the Moon with lava on it!). Each level will have 2 more Thargoids than the last.
